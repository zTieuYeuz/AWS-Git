Create VPC

Create VPC Lab VPC

  1. Go to VPC service management console
    • Click Your VPC.
    • Click Create VPC.

  1. At the Create VPC page.
    • In the Name tag field, enter Lab VPC.
    • In the IPv4 CIDR field, enter: 10.10.0.0/16.
    • Click Create VPC.
